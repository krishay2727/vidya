Web-based flight simulator · Arduino joystick control
Arduino wiring: Joystick 1 (pitch/roll) → A0 (roll), A1 (pitch). Joystick 2 (throttle/yaw) → A2 (yaw), A3 (throttle). Flash the included flightsim_arduino.ino sketch, plug in via USB, then click Connect and select the Arduino's serial port.

Keyboard fallback: W/S pitch · A/D roll · Q/E yaw · Shift/Ctrl throttle · R reset. Requires Chrome or Edge for Arduino connection (Web Serial API).
